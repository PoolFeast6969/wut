About Us

Eastern Animal Rescue is a registered not-for-profit animal welfare organisation that had offered refuge and a second chance to stray and abandoned dogs since 2002. Eastern Animal Rescue is set apart by its dynamic and positive attitude to the problems that face domestic animals in our society today, actively seeking to find solutions rather than simply pick up the pieces. EAR offers the following services:

  • Adopt-A-Dog Program - Relocate stray or unwanted pets into new homes.
  • Animal Rehabilitation - Retrain mistreated or surrendered dogs to enable rehousing via the "Adopt-A-Dog" program.
